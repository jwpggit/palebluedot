PALEBLUEDOT.STUDIO is a creative collective exploring visual communication. Our planet seems complicated, chaotic, and sometimes full of hatred. When we look at our planet at a distance, it is a lonely, but precious speck in the great enveloping cosmic dark. Inspired by the astrophysicist Carl Sagan’s poetic philosophy, we pursue the simplification of complicated and convoluted things. We cross boundaries through design, graphics, fashion, photography, video, music, architecture, and food. We believe our work can mediate disputes, hatred, and misunderstanding on our planet.
